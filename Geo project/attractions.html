Attractions!

Tourism is also a major human activity in the Pacific Maritime ecozone. In the more populated cities like Victoria and Vancouver, there are many man-made attractions as well as some physical ones. Some of the Man-made tourist attractions are the Vancouver lookout and the Royal British Columbia Museum in Victoria. Some of the Physical tourist attractions are the Vandusen Botanical Garden located in Vancouver, and the Stanley park, which is also in Vancouver.
