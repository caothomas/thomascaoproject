Welcome to the Pacific Maritime!

Welcome to the Pacific Maritime, one of Canada's 15 ecozones. The Pacific Maritime ecozone lies along the coast of British Columbia and goes slightly into the Yukon Territory. The ecozone also narrows on land as it is closer to Yukon territories. The Pacific Maritime ecozone also includes the islands of British Columbia. In this website I will talk about the climate, landforms, soil, human activity, vegetation, and wildlife along with 2 cities in the Pacific Maritime ecozone.
