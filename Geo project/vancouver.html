Vancouver!

Geography

Vancouver city is a coastal, seaport city in the mainlands of British Columbia with Vancouver island shielding it from the Pacific Ocean. Vancouver has a population of around 631,416 people and has an area of 114 square kilometers. There is an inlet know as the Burrard inlet that separates North and West Vancouver. Vancouver also has many parks like the Stanly park and the Vandusen Botanical Garden along with Fraser River right next to it.

Climate

Vancouver has a moderate, Maritime climate. Vancouver city is protected by the coast Mountains and also warmed by the warm ocean currents, making Vancouver one of the warmest cities in Canada. Vancouver is also one of the most rainiest cities in Canada but it does not get as much precipitation as other coastal cities due to Vancouver island shielding Vancouver city from the Pacific Ocean, but it does receive more sunshine than other coastal cities. November and December is Vancouver's wettest months with an average of 182 millimeters of precipitation.
